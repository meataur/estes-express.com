Bill of Lading Templates

The BOL Template section shows all saved Bill of Lading templates created for this My Estes account. You can sort the columns by clicking one of the arrows that appears when you hover over the column headers for Template Name, BOL Reference #, BOL Date, PRO #, Shipper, and Consignee. To create a totally new template, simply choose to save your document as a template after you complete a Bill of Lading in the Create BOL section of the application.
