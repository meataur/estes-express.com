Additional Cargo Liability Coverage Guidelines

Standard Liability
Be sure to consider whether you need to purchase additional coverage. As per Item 350 of our Estes Rules Tariff EXLA 105, Estes' standard liability on spot quotes cannot exceed $2.00 per pound/per article for new products or equipment. The liability limit for anything used OR new and uncrated is $0.10 per pound/per article. Estes' standard maximum liability is $10,000 per trailer.

Full Value Coverage (FVC)
If you need additional liability coverage, indicate the amount desired in the applicable field. Please note that quotes for Volume and Truckload are the only ones that include the cost of FVC when coverage is requested in this quote application. Cost is added for other service levels at the time of billing. Please refer to Item 350 of the Estes Rules Tariff EXLA 105 for complete details. Remember, to obtain coverage, you must write the amount of Full Value Coverage you are requesting on your Bill of Lading.
